Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At PoliSpectrum, we believe in radical transparency and user privacy. This page explains our "No-Harvesting" data policy and how we handle your interaction with our site.

The Core Promise:

We do not collect, store, sell, or trade your political test results, personal answers, or any identifying information.

1. No Data Harvesting

Unlike many political alignment tests, we do not store your answers in a database. Your results are calculated locally in your browser. Once you close your browser tab, your specific test answers are gone forever.

2. Cookies and Tracking

In this current version of the site, we do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics that identify you personally. We may use standard web server logs (provided by our host, GitHub Pages) which record basic non-identifying info like your IP address for security purposes.

3. External Links

Our site contains links to other websites (like FAQ resources or example scores). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

4. Future Updates

If we implement Google Ads or more advanced analytics in the future to keep the site running, this policy will be updated immediately to reflect those changes, and a notification will be posted on the homepage.
